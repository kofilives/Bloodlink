1

Registration

You will complete a simple registration form with your personal details. Remember to bring your picture ID for easy identification.

2

Screening

Our nurses will take you through a brief medical check so that you or the person who receives your blood are not negatively affected after donation.

3

Donation

Once you are found fit, you will be directed to our comfortable donor bed to give blood. This usually takes between 10-15 minutes.

4

Refreshment

When you are done giving the blood, our staff will take care of you to make sure you are safe to go back to normal life. You will also receive a slight refreshment as you rest
